What is the GDP gap calculator?
The GDP gap calculator is a tool that measures the output gap that is the percentage difference between actual GDP and its potential level.
How to calculate GDP gap?
To calculate GDP gap you should use the following formula:
GDP gap = ((actual GDP - potential GDP) / potential GDP) * 100
Why use GDP gap calculator?
The GDP gap calculator is used in order to compute the output gap.
